## ‚ö° 5-MINUTE OVERVIEW

**The Pre-Sell Methodology (NOT Build ‚Üí Sell):**
1. **Reach out** to 5-10 ideal members (don't build yet)
2. **Sell on outcome** via serve calls (not features)
3. **Get money** + hear objections
4. **Build based on feedback** (what they actually need)

**Why this works:**
- Validates demand BEFORE building
- Surfaces real objections early
- Builds WITH customers, not FOR them
- Faster to revenue

**Key principle:** "You sell 100x better on a serve call than any sales page."

## üìû PHASE 1: PRE-SELL (Week 1-2)

### Step 1: Identify Beta Candidates

**Who to reach out to:**
- [ ] People who've expressed interest
- [ ] Warm network (know/like/trust you)
- [ ] Ideal avatar match
- [ ] Can afford pricing
- [ ] Will give honest feedback

**How many:** 5-10 people (not 50)

**Why this number:**
- Small enough to serve personally
- Large enough to validate demand
- Can handle on serve calls
- Won't overwhelm you

---

### Step 2: Reach Out (Conversation-Based)

**Method:** Direct message, not mass email

**Outreach template:**
```
Hey [Name],

Quick question: I'm launching [transformation promise] for [target avatar].

It's [format] at [price]/month, starting [date].

Would this be valuable to you? Want to jump on a quick call to talk through it?

[Your Name]
```

**Key elements:**
- Personal (not blast)
- Specific (transformation, not features)
- Low commitment ask (call, not "buy now")
- Timing clear

**Real example (Austin):**
> Reached out to 5-10 chiropractors from Growth Summit
> "I'm helping 25-35K/month practices hit 50K WITHOUT diluting services. 
> Want to chat?"

---

### Step 3: Serve Calls (NOT Sales Calls)

**Goal:** Understand their problem, show how you'd solve it, handle objections, get commitment

**Serve call structure (30-45 min):**

**1. Discovery (10 min):**
```
"Tell me about your situation. Where are you at right now with [specific goal]?"

[Listen, take notes, capture exact language]
```

**2. Positioning (15 min):**
```
"Okay, here's what I'm thinking. I'd help you [transformation] by:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

The format is [membership/cohort], and it's [price]/month.

Does that sound like it would help?"
```

**3. Objection handling (10 min):**
```
"What concerns do you have? What would need to be true for this to be a 
no-brainer?"

[Address objections one at a time]
```

**4. Close (5 min):**
```
"So, are you in? Can I count you as one of the founding members?"

[If yes:]
"Awesome. I'll send you a payment link today. We start [date]."
```

**Key principle:** Handle objections ONE AT A TIME, move toward decision.

**Real example (Jordan's approach):**
> "You sell 100x better than a sales page. Get on calls, hear objections 
> live, adjust positioning real-time."

---

### Step 4: Get Money (Payment BEFORE Build)

**Don't:** Wait to build before charging

**Do:** Get payment, THEN build based on their input

**Payment setup:**
- [ ] Create Circle paywall link (simple)
- [ ] Set founding member price
- [ ] Send link after serve call
- [ ] Collect payment

**Why money first:**
- Validates real commitment (not just interest)
- Gives you runway to build properly
- Makes them invested in success
- Surfaces serious objections vs. polite interest

**Script:**
```
"Great! I'm sending you the payment link now. Once you're in, I'll add you 
to the founding member group where we'll co-create this together."
```

---

### Step 5: Capture Objections & Feedback

**After each serve call, document:**
- [ ] What objections came up?
- [ ] What pricing concerns?
- [ ] What format questions?
- [ ] What deliverables did they ask about?
- [ ] What language resonated?
- [ ] What didn't land?

**Use this to refine:**
- Positioning
- Pricing
- Deliverables
- Messaging

**Pattern:** Build based on REAL feedback, not assumptions.

## üîë KEY FRAMEWORKS

### 1. Pre-Sell Methodology (GrowthCommunity Standard)

**Sequence:**
```
TRADITIONAL (Wrong):
Build ‚Üí Launch ‚Üí Sell ‚Üí Iterate
‚Üì
PROBLEMS:
- Built wrong thing
- No validation
- Wasted time/money
- Hard to pivot

GROWTHCOMMUNITY (Right):
Reach out ‚Üí Sell ‚Üí Build ‚Üí Iterate
‚Üì
BENEFITS:
- Validated demand
- Real objections surfaced
- Built with customers
- Faster to revenue
```

---

### 2. Serve Calls > Landing Pages

**Concept:** Sell via conversation, not sales page

**Why:**
- You sell 100x better than copy
- Can handle objections live
- Adjust positioning real-time
- Build relationship immediately

**When to use sales page:**
- After 20-30 serve calls (you know all objections)
- Scaling beyond your call capacity
- Inbound leads from content

**For beta:** Always start with serve calls.

---

### 3. Work ON Calls (Execution Model)

**Concept:** Don't assign homework, do work together

**Format:**
- 15 min teaching
- 75 min doing (together)
- Immediate help when stuck
- Tangible progress each session

**Why this works:**
- Accountability (they actually do it)
- Momentum (progress every week)
- Community (see each other work)
- Value (help when they need it)

---

### 4. Network vs. Transformation Model

**Two approaches:**

**Transformation Model:**
- Specific outcome (lose 20 lbs, launch business)
- Members "graduate" after achieving
- High churn by design
- Content/education focused

**Network Model:**
- Ongoing access to peers/experts
- No "graduation"
- Lower churn potential
- Connection/resources focused

**Decision:** Which fits your offer?

## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON MISTAKES

**Mistake 1: Building before validating**
- Spend 6 weeks building, nobody buys
- Solution: Pre-sell FIRST, build SECOND

**Mistake 2: Perfect landing page syndrome**
- Obsess over copy/design instead of selling
- Solution: Use Circle paywall link, serve calls only

**Mistake 3: Creating too much content up front**
- 100 posts nobody asked for
- Solution: Seed 1 week, add based on needs

**Mistake 4: Homework instead of execution**
- "Go do this and report back"
- Solution: Work ON calls with immediate help

**Mistake 5: Complex automation too early**
- Fancy workflows before knowing what works
- Solution: Manual first, automate later
